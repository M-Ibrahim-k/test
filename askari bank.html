Project Name and Duration

Askari Bank – Mobile Banking Redesign (Practice Project)
Duration: 1 week (April 2025)

My Role

UX/UI Designer – Responsible for user research, wireframing, prototyping, and UI design using Figma.

Project Goal

The goal was to modernize and simplify the mobile banking experience for Askari Bank users. The existing design appeared cluttered and unintuitive, especially for first-time users. This redesign aims to improve usability, visual hierarchy, and accessibility for a smoother banking experience.

Research & Insights

While going through the original Askari Bank app, I noticed that the design felt quite cluttered and heavy. So, I decided to dig deeper by checking user feedback on app stores and forums. Here’s what stood out:

  • Too much going on: Screens were packed with information, which made it hard for users to focus on what really mattered.
  • No clear flow: Important actions and info weren’t placed in an order that made sense, so things felt a bit all over the place.

With all that in mind, I went for a clean and minimal design that’s easier on the eyes and helps users get things done without feeling overwhelmed.

Intended Audience

Primary Users: Everyday banking customers (ages 20–50) who use the mobile app for balance checks, fund transfers, and bill payments. Secondary Users: Elderly customers who value clarity, simplicity, and security in digital experiences.

Sketches or Wireframes

User Testing Results

Since this was a practice project, informal feedback was gathered from peers familiar with mobile banking apps. Key takeaways:

  • Navigation flow: Users appreciated the improved navigation structure and logical grouping of features.
  • Visual design: The modern UI and consistent styling made the app feel more trustworthy and easier to use.
  • Clarity: Clear CTAs and better information hierarchy led to faster task completion in the prototype.

Final Design

Conclusion

This project was an excellent opportunity to explore design improvements for financial apps in Pakistan. It deepened my understanding of how simplicity and clarity play a key role in mobile banking. If given real user data and access to stakeholder feedback, this design could be evolved into a functional concept with measurable impact.
